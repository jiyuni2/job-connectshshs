# JobConnect - Find Your Dream Job in Somalia

JobConnect is the leading job platform in Somalia, connecting job seekers with employers across major cities including Hargeisa, Borama, Berbera, Gabilay, and Burco.

## 🌟 Features

### For Job Seekers
- **Browse Jobs**: Search through hundreds of job listings
- **Advanced Filters**: Filter by city, job type, experience level, salary range
- **Save Jobs**: Keep track of interesting opportunities
- **Easy Applications**: Apply to jobs with just a few clicks
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Connect directly with employers via WhatsApp

### For Employers
- **Post Jobs**: Reach thousands of qualified candidates
- **Manage Applications**: Review and respond to job applications
- **Applicant Tracking**: Keep track of all candidates in one place
- **Accept/Reject Applications**: Streamline your hiring process

### For Admins
- **Complete Control**: Manage all jobs, users, and applications
- **User Management**: Oversee all registered users
- **Content Moderation**: Ensure quality job postings
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Track platform usage and statistics
